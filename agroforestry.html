Agroforestry And Sustainable Farming

ENCO uganda promotes agroforestry practices, encouraging farmers to intergrate trees into their farming systems to improve soil fertility, enhance crop yields, and restore forest cover.

Farmers are also trained in organic farming techniques, such as making organic manure and pesticides at home, to reduce production costs and promote environmentally sustainable agriculture.
