Tree Planting

We at Enco, we use tree planting as a powerful community initiative to combat deforestation and promote sustainability. Our approach begins with engaging local communities in awareness and education programs about the importance of trees for the environment. We then establish tree nurseries, providing native tree species that are crucial for biodiversity. Working closely with community leaders, we identify degraded areas that need reforestation and mobilize volunteers for planting drives.

In collaboration with local schools, farmers, and other stakeholders, we plant trees to restore ecosystems, improve soil health, and absorb carbon emissions. Regular monitoring and evaluation ensure that the trees thrive, creating long-term impact. By fostering community ownership of these projects, we not only restore the environment but also provide economic benefits, such as sustainable agroforestry and ecotourism opportunities. Through tree planting, Enco empowers communities to protect their natural resources and build resilience against climate change.
