Engaging Community in Income Generating activities

To reduce the community's dependence on environmentally destructive practices, ENCO Uganda promotes alternative income-generating activities, such as community tourism, savings, beekeeping, and small-scale commercial farming. Through these initiatives, we foster a cycle where economic prosperity and environmental stewardship go hand-in-hand, creating a healthier environment and resilient communities for future generations.
