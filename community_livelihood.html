Community Livelihood

Promoting community livelihood activities like agriculture is a core focus of ENCO's mission to uplift local communities while ensuring environmental sustainability.

ENCO, actively supports and promotes sustainable agricultural practices to enhance food security, generate income, and improve overall livelihoods within the community. By providing training, resources, and access to eco-friendly farming techniques, we empower individuals to grow their own food, improve their financial well-being, and reduce their environmental impact. This approach not only boosts economic resilience but also fosters a deeper connection between community members and their natural surroundings, ensuring that livelihoods and conservation thrive together.
