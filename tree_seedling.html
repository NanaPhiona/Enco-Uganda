One of our key initiatives is the "Tree Seedling Distributions" program, aimed at empowering communities to take an active role in reforestation and environmental conservation.

By distributing a diverse range of indigenous tree seedlings to local farmers, schools, and community groups, we are helping to combat deforestation, enhance biodiversity, and mitigate the effects of climate change. These seedlings, carefully selected for their ability to thrive in local ecosystems, not only contribute to the restoration of degraded lands but also provide long-term economic benefits through agroforestry and sustainable farming practices. The initiative is a cornerstone of our community-based conservation efforts, fostering environmental stewardship and promoting a greener future for generations to come.

Through this program, ENCO is driving a transformative movement that bridges the gap between nature conservation and community empowerment, ensuring that each tree planted becomes a symbol of hope, resilience, and environmental responsibility. Join us in our mission to create a sustainable world, where every seedling planted today grows into a legacy of environmental harmony tomorrow.
