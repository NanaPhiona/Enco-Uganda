Bee Keeping For Conservation and Community Livelihood

At Environmental and Nature Conservation Uganda, we promote beekeeping as a sustainable practice that supports both environmental conservation and community livelihoods. By engaging in beekeeping, communities are provided with an alternative source of income, reducing their reliance on activities that may harm the environment, such as deforestation. This practice helps preserve existing forests and promotes biodiversity.

Beekeeping also offers a valuable source of nutritious food through honey production, further enhancing the livelihoods of community members. By supporting this eco-friendly enterprise, we ensure that local people can thrive economically while contributing to the conservation of our natural resources.

We perform bee keeping through enganging communities in the projects
