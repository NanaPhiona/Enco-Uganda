Village SACCO and Financial Literacy

We are supporting communities by providing them with financial literacy training and teaching them how to effectively save money through village SACCOs (Savings and Credit Cooperative Organizations). These programs empower individuals to manage their finances, build savings, and access small loans to invest in income-generating activities such as sustainable agriculture and small businesses.

By fostering a culture of savings and financial management, we enable communities to improve their economic resilience, achieve greater financial independence, and ultimately make a lasting difference in their lives. This approach strengthens both livelihoods and conservation efforts, ensuring long-term community growth and sustainability.
