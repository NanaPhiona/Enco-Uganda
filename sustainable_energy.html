Sustainable Energy Stoves

At ENCO Uganda, we intergrate sustainable energy solutions as part of our environmental conservation efforts. One of the key approaches we promote is the use of energy-saving stoves. These stoves are designed to significantly reduce the amount of firewood and charcoal for cooking, thereby lessening the pressure on forests and decreasing deforestation.

In addition to conserving forests, energy-saving stoves provide economic benefits to communities. By using less fuel, families can save a substantial amount money that would otherwise be spent on firewood or charcoal. This extra savings can then be redirected towards other essential needs, further enhancing community livelihoods.

Through the promotion of energy-saving stoves, we not only support environmental conservation but also help improve the economic well-being of communities we serve.
